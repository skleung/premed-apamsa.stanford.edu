Mission Statement

We are an organization of Asian and Pacific Islander (APA) pre-medical students committed to addressing the unique health challenges of APA communities through education, outreach, advocacy, and service.

About us

APAMSA officially started in 1995 with the now annual APAMSA conference. The pre-medical chapter of APAMSA at Stanford was founded at the end of Fall 2013, and we're looking to officialy kick-off recruiting during Spring 2014.

Join our mailing list or apply here by 5/4/14 if you're interested in joining us!
